Deep Cleaning

Paint, glass, emblems, gas lids, and plastics/rubbers are properly cleaned and decontaminated with an iron remover, followed by a clay bar treatment.

Glass

The exterior glass is decontaminated, and the windshield is protected with Rain X or Invisible Glass.

Ceramic Coating

Paint is coated with a 1-year layer of ceramic coating, providing chemical resistance, durability, hydrophobic properties, and a glossy finish.

Tires

Tires, wheels, and wheel wells are deeply cleaned. Wheel wells and tires are dressed.

Polish

Paint receives a single-stage polish to safely remove swirls, defects, and minor scratches.

Plastics

All black plastics will be restored with a protectant.

⚠ After coating is applied, do not wash the vehicle for 5-7 days.
